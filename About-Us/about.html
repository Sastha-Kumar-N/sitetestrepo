Our vision is to create a comprehensive and accessible repository of genomic information for indigenous species from every corner of Bharat. We believe that by unraveling the genetic code of these remarkable organisms, we can contribute to the preservation of biodiversity, foster scientific discovery, and support conservation efforts worldwide. 

Why Indigenous Species Matter 

Indigenous species are the bedrock of our ecosystems. They have evolved over millennia, adapting to their unique environments and playing crucial roles in maintaining ecological balance. However, these species are facing unprecedented challenges, from habitat loss and climate change to poaching and invasive species. Understanding the genomes of indigenous species is pivotal to their conservation. It equips us with the knowledge needed to develop effective conservation strategies, mitigate threats, and restore ecosystems that have been disrupted. 

Our Commitment 

1. Data Accessibility: We are committed to open science and data accessibility. Our database is freely accessible to researchers, conservationists, educators, and anyone passionate about preserving biodiversity.

 2. Scientific Excellence: Our team of dedicated scientists and experts employ cutting-edge genomic technologies and rigorous research methodologies to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the data we provide. 

3. Conservation Partnerships: We actively collaborate with indigenous communities, conservation organizations, and governments to promote responsible and ethical research that respects the rights and traditions of local communities. 

4. Education and Outreach: We believe that knowledge is a powerful tool for change. Through educational initiatives and public outreach programs, we aim to raise awareness about the importance of indigenous species and genomics in conservation.
